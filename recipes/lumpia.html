Lumpia Recipe

What is Lumpia?

Lumpia is a Filipino spring roll made with a thin wrapper filled with a mixture of ground meat, vegetables, and seasonings. Itâ€™s typically fried until golden and crispy, served as an appetizer o snack with a dipping sauce like sweet chili or vinegar.

Ingredients

  • Lumpia wrappers
  • Ground meat (pork or chicken)
  • Vegetables (carrots, onions, garlic)
  • Seasonings (soy sauce, salt, pepper)
  • Dipping sauce (optional)
  • Oil

Steps to make Lumpia

  1. Prepare Filling: Mix ground meat, chopped vegetables, and seasonings.
  2. Wrap Lumpia: Place filling on lumpia wrapper, roll tightly, and seal edges.
  3. Fry Rolls: Deep-fry until golden and crispy.
  4. Drain Oil: Place cooked lumpia on paper towels to remove excess oil.
  5. Serve: Enjoy with your favorite dipping sauce.
