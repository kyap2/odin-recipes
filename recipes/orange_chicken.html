Orange Chicken Recipe

What is Orange Chicken?

Orange chicken is a Chinese-American dish of fried chicken coated in a sweet, tangy orange sauce. Popular in restaurants like Panda Express, it's loved for its crispy texture and bold citrusy flavor.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Orange Juice
  • Soy Sauce
  • Sugar
  • Vinegar
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • Oil

Steps to make Orange Chicken

  1. Prepare chicken: Cut into bite-sized pieces and coat in a cornstarch and flour mixture
  2. Fry Chicken: Deep-fry until golden and crispy, then drain on paper towels
  3. Make Sauce: Combine orange juice, soy sauce, sugar vinegar, ginger, and garlic in a pan. Simmer until slightly thickened
  4. Coat Chicken: Toss fried chicken in the sauce until fully coated
  5. Serve: Enjoy hot with rice or noodles
