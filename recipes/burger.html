Burger Recipe

What is a Burger?

A hamburger is a classic sandwich consisting of a seasoned, cooked ground beef patty served in a sliced bun. Often topped with lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, cheese, and condiments like ketchup or mustard, it's a versatile and popular dish enjoyed worldwide, with countless variations to suit different tastes.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef patty
  • Burger buns
  • Lecttuce, tomato, onion
  • Cheese
  • Pickle
  • Condiments
  • Salt
  • Oil

Steps to make a Burger

  1. Shape Patties: Form ground beef into evenly sized patties and season with salt and pepper.
  2. Toast Buns: Lightly toast the burger buns on a grill or in a pan.
  3. Assemble Burger: Layer lettuce, tomato, onion, and optional toppings on the bottom bun, followed by the patty.
  4. Coat Chicken: Toss fried chicken in the sauce until fully coated
  5. Add Condiments: Spread condiments on the top bun, place it over the patty, and serve.
